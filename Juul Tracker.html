When a craving hits

The 4 D's — do one

Delay — set a 10-min timer. Cravings peak in 3–5 min, then fade.

Drink — cold water, slow sips. Resets the oral thing.

Deep breathe — 4 in, 7 hold, 8 out. Four rounds.

Distract — draw, walk, cold shower, push-ups.

Watch out for these

Between clients — biggest trigger. Keep gum in your pocket.

After meals — walk outside for 5 min first, then decide.

Driving — put the pod in the trunk, not the console.

Stress spikes — say it out loud: "I'm stressed." Then breathe.
